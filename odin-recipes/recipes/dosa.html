Recipe: Dosa

Description

Dosa is a crepe style food that is made in South Indian cuisine. The batter is created through ground black gram and rice. It is cooked on a griddle with oil until it is crispy and golden brown. It is usually served with chutney or/and sambar.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 3/4 to 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon chana dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tablespoons poha
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps:

  1. First wash and soak urad dal, rice, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds
  2. Drain the water from all of these
  3. Grind until a smooth, bubbly batter is formed
  4. Put dosa batter into a warm place (or oven with light on) to ferment for 5 to 16 hours, until it becomes bubbly
